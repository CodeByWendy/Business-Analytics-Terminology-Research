# Business Analytics Terminology Project

## Overview

This project is a research-based glossary of 38 key Business Analytics concepts. It focuses on understanding how data is used to support decision-making in real business environments.

Each term includes a clear definition and a simple real-world example.

---

## Objectives

* Understand core Business Analytics concepts
* Differentiate between similar terms (e.g., KPI vs Metric)
* Apply concepts to real-world scenarios

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. Foundational Concepts

* Business Analytics
* Data Analytics
* Business Intelligence (BI)
* Big Data
* Data-Driven Decision Making
* Types of Analytics (Descriptive, Diagnostic, Predictive, Prescriptive)
* Data Literacy

### 2. Data, Measurement & Reporting

* Structured vs Unstructured Data
* Data Warehouse
* Data Mining
* Data Visualization
* Dashboard
* Metrics & KPIs
* Benchmark
* Segmentation

### 3. Customer & Sales KPIs

* Conversion Rate
* Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Churn Rate & Retention Rate
* Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Sales Funnel

### 4. Marketing & Campaign Analytics

* Marketing Campaign
* Post-Campaign Analysis
* Incremental Revenue
* Return on Investment (ROI)
* Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
* Click-Through Rate (CTR)
* Bounce Rate
* A/B Testing
* Lead Generation
* Marketing Attribution
